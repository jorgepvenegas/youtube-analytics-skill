## Tips for Writing Strong Findings

1. **Lead with the insight, not the data.** "Tutorials convert 3x better" is better than "The subs/view metric for tutorials was 0.9%."
2. **Quantify everything.** Use ratios, multiples, and percentages.
3. **Distinguish correlation from causation.** Use "associated with" or "linked to" when you can't prove causation.
4. **Be honest about data limitations.** If a segment has only 2 videos, flag it as "insufficient data" rather than drawing conclusions.
5. **Make recommendations specific.** "Improve hooks" is vague. "Add a problem-statement hook in the first 15 seconds for tutorial videos" is actionable.
